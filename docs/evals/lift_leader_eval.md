# LIFT Leader Evaluation

## Purpose

Evaluate whether LIFT leader identification distinguishes true sector leaders
from late followers or one-day spikes.

## Rubric

- Lead: earliest launch or repair.
- Impact: can move or cool the sector.
- Flow: sustained and healthy liquidity.
- Trend: stronger structure than peers and sector.

## Error Types

- `WRONG_LEADER`
- `NO_EVIDENCE`
- `OVERCONFIDENT`
